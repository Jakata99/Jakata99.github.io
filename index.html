A decision towards change!

Like many things in life, it all happened completely by chance. I was on a vacation in Spain visiting relatives. At this time, I was a fresh graduate from university, still trying to figure out what I want to be doing. Who could guess that a conversation with my cousin would make me consider to try out something completely different. After some time, I decided to give programming a try, and signed for SoftUni without previous any experience. One week into it, and I already loved it. After few months studying while working full time in London, I decided to go back to Bulgaria permanently. This was a changing moment for me, as I can now dedicate fully. It took me until mid-april until I convinced myself to specialise in the front-end web development field. As of today (6th June), I am still fully-commiting my time in learning new things and improving with a speed that I can handle.
